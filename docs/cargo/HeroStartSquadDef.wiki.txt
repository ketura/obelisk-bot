One row per slot in a hero's starting army. Each hero has 1-3 '''primary''' slots (<code>variant=primary</code>) and 1 '''alt''' slot (<code>variant=alt</code>). Across the 108 faction heroes in the 2026-05-03 corpus: 100 have 3 primary slots, 5 have 2, 3 have 1; all 108 have exactly 1 alt slot.

The bot emits these rows on the parent <code>Data:Hero/<id></code> page, after the <code><nowiki>{{HeroDef}}</nowiki></code> and <code><nowiki>{{TranslationDef}}</nowiki></code> blocks.

== Field notes ==
* '''Primary key is <code>(hero_id, variant, slot)</code>'''.
* '''<code>variant</code>''' distinguishes the source's parallel arrays <code>startSquad</code> (primary) and <code>startSquadAlt</code> (alt). Source data has alt as always exactly 1 slot of the form <code>(unit_sid, min=1, max=1)</code> in the 2026-05-03 corpus, but the schema preserves min/max in case future patches diverge.
* '''<code>unit_sid</code>''' references a <code>Unit.id</code> (e.g. <code>esquire</code>, <code>crossbowman</code>, <code>griffin</code>).
* '''<code>min</code>/<code>max</code>''' define the random count range for that slot at battle start. Primary slots have meaningful ranges (e.g. 14-20 esquires); alt slots are typically 1-1.
