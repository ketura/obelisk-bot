Splinter for <code>ability_type = active</code>. The biggest of the splinter tables — every active ability has a dealer, target params, and typically buff payload, plus optional damage tuning and shoot parameters.

Join to <code>UnitAbilityDef</code> on <code>ability_id</code>.

== Field notes ==
* '''<code>cd</code> sentinels:''' <code>-1</code> = once per battle (one charge regenerates only through specific abilities); <code>0</code> = no cooldown; positive = rounds.
* '''<code>action_cost</code>:''' defaults to 1 (= consumes the unit's turn). <code>0</code> means the unit retains its action — wiki templates should display this as something like "free action" rather than rendering "0".
* '''<code>stat_dmg_mult</code>''' is a multiplier on basic-attack damage. <code>1.0</code> = standard damage, <code>0.5</code> = halved (e.g. ranged AoE), <code>2.0</code> = doubled. When the field is missing on the source JSON, the engine treats it as <code>1.0</code>.
'' ''*<code>buff_*</code> fields** are sparse — only populated for abilities that apply a buff (~25% of active abilities). The buff's actual stats live in <code>DB/buffs/</code>, joined via <code>buff_sid</code>. '' ''*<code>shoot_*</code> fields** apply mostly to the arbitrator family (paradoxical-shot mechanic) and a handful of other ranged abilities.
* '''<code>cast_target_tags</code> / <code>affect_target_tags</code>''' are <code>List (,) of String</code> in Cargo — comma-separated in wiki, exposed as multi-row joins on Cargo's auxiliary <code>__values</code> table when queried.
