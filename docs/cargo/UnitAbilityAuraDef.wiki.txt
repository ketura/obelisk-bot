== Field notes ==
* '''<code>target</code>''' controls who the aura affects. <code>allynoself</code> excludes the emitting unit (e.g. Esquire's aura buffs only neighbors).
* '''<code>power</code>''' is currently always <code>1</code> in the corpus — the column is here as a future-proof. Higher values would mean multiple stacks of the aura's effect on each affected unit.
* '''<code>radius</code>''' is currently always <code>1</code> (adjacent hexes). Aura units are short-range battlefield-shapers.
* '''<code>tag</code>''' is the aura family name. Six distinct values in 2026-05-03: <code>angel_aura</code>, <code>black_dragon_aura</code>, <code>ent_aura</code>, <code>esquire_aura</code>, <code>fairy_dragon_aura</code>, <code>qilin_aura</code>.
* '''<code>affected_stat</code> / <code>affected_stat_amount</code>''' describe the stat the aura applies. May be missing for auras whose effect is a non-stat payload (currently fairy_dragon's <code>alwaysMinDmg: true</code> pattern would hit this — encoded as a bool, not a numeric stat, so the bot omits the <code>_amount</code> slot).
